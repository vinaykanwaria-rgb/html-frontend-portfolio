About Me

Aspiring Educator

An aspiring educator committed to fostering academic excellence, critical thinking, and holistic student development through innovative and student-centered teaching practices. I hold a Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed.) from CDLU University and have successfully qualified both the CTET (Central Teacher Eligibility Test) and PTET (Pre-Teacher Education Test) 2026.


My teaching journey has been strengthened through practical classroom experience at Arya Vidhya Mandir Senior Secondary School, where I developed and delivered engaging lesson plans, managed classroom activities, and supported students in their academic and personal growth. I believe that every child has unique potential, and my goal is to create a positive, inclusive, and motivating learning environment that encourages curiosity, confidence, and lifelong learning.


I possess strong skills in classroom management, lesson planning, student engagement, communication, and educational technology. With a student-centered approach and a commitment to continuous professional development, I strive to make learning meaningful, enjoyable, and impactful.
